How to play
Lidude Isle

Your guide to the happiest, fuzziest, most addictive little island on Lido. Collect 21 species of Lidude, staff your stations, and build a tiny empire that hums along 24/7 — even when your tab is closed.

The basics

The 60-second version ⏱️

Everything below is sauce. These five loops are the entire game.

1

🪙 Tap to collect

Stations fill up in real time. Come back often or they'll cap out and stop earning.

2

🎰 Pull Lidudes

Open crates. Assign creatures to matching stations for a 2× affinity bonus.

3

⬆️ Upgrade stations

More tiers unlock more slots, more storage, and exponentially more output.

4

🔥 Daily streak & quests

Login every day. Don't break the streak. Finish three quests for a bonus chest.

5

🎁 Free crate, every 4h

One free pull on the house. Claim it the moment the button pulses orange.

The onboarding

Your first 10 minutes 🌅

You'll land with 200 🪙, 15 💎, 50 🌾, a starter Bloomy, and a starter Coiny. Here's what to do, in order.

1

👆 Tap your Farm and your Mine

Watch the little +N 🪙 particles puff upward. That's the core reward loop — the dopamine drip. Tap a few times. Feel that? That's hours of your future life.

2

🎰 Do your first pull

You have exactly enough for one basic pull (250 🪙). Do it. Even if it lands Common, you've just nudged the pity counter closer to a guaranteed Epic by pull 10, Legendary by 50, and Mythic by 200.

3

🪑 Assign your new Lidude

Open Lidudes, tap the new one, hit Assign. Check its Affinity — Lidudes produce 2× at their preferred station type. A Spark ⚡ in a Mine is worth two Sparks in a Farm.

4

🎁 Claim the free crate

Hit the pulsing orange button, top right. One free pull, and the 4-hour clock starts ticking. This is your main return hook — try to claim it the moment it rings.

5

🔥 Start your streak

The streak badge on the left side of the island is tracking you. Day 7 hands you a free premium pull. Miss a day and it all resets. The Farmville people invented this for a reason.

6

📜 Finish a quest

You have three daily tasks. The "Tap stations" one is usually two minutes of taps. Free 🪙, free XP, free gems. Claim all three for a bonus chest on top.

By minute 10: 3-5 Lidudes, a Tier-2 station, and a strong urge to see what Legendary looks like.

The engine

The core loop 🔁

Your isle runs on stations — passive generators that accumulate resources in a storage buffer. When the buffer fills, production stops. That's the whole engine.

Assigning Lidudes stacks output. A station's base rate gets multiplied by every Lidude you slot in, and affinity-matched Lidudes count double. A Tier-3 Mine with five mine-affinity Lidudes will dramatically outproduce a Tier-3 Mine with five random ones.

Pro moves

Tips for new isle-keepers 💡

Little habits that compound into big numbers.

🎯 Don't spread thin

Two affinity-matched Lidudes in one station beat four random ones across two. Specialize early.

⬆️ Upgrade before you expand

A Tier-3 station with full slots earns more than two Tier-1s. Upgrades multiply output, they don't add linearly.

🔟 Save gems for 10-pulls

The 10-pull is 10% cheaper than ten singles. And the big-reveal animation at the end is, objectively, better.

♊ Duplicates aren't useless

A duplicate means you can staff two stations with the same species. Don't (and can't) sell them — you'll want them.

👀 Tap the visitor instantly

See a glowing Lidude walking past? Drop everything. Legendary visitors can skip 30+ pulls of grind.

📖 Compendium is the long game

Filling all 21 slots unlocks the (coming soon) prestige bonus. Even Commons count — don't skip them.

🟢 Collect when caps

When a station bar turns green and pulses, it's full and earning nothing. Every minute it sits full is wasted.

🎰 Always start pity

Even a losing Common pull advances your guaranteed Epic countdown. Pity is always earning for you in the background.

Advanced

The "refresh loop" 🔄

Once you're past Tier 2, this is the 90-second rhythm that maxes your coin-per-hour.

1Open the app.
2Tap every station # pockets storage + kicks 2× multiplier
3Claim free crate if ready
4Claim any completed quests
5If 250+ coins, do one basic pull # advances pity
6Close the app. Come back in 2-4 hours.
Total time: ~90 seconds. Result: roughly optimal play. Do this six times a day and you're in the top 5%.

Questions

FAQ ❓

The stuff everyone asks.

Does my isle earn while I'm offline?
Yes. Up to 8 hours of offline production accumulates in your stations. When you return, half is auto-collected (an instant welcome gift) and half waits in storage for you to tap.
Where's my save?
Locally, in your browser. Clearing site data wipes your progress. Cloud saves tied to your Lido account are coming in v2.
What's the Compendium for?
Right now: bragging rights and a discovery XP bump on new species. Post-prestige, it'll control which permanent bonuses you can equip.
How rare is Cosmo 🌌 really?
0.05% per pull. Pity guarantees one by pull 200. Most players will see their first Mythic somewhere between pulls 80 and 200. The first one is unforgettable.
Is there an end?
Technically no — the prestige system will let the game run forever. Practically, most players use the Compendium as their "done" marker.
Can I lose progress?
Only by clearing browser data. Resources and Lidudes can never be removed from your account. Streaks can reset if you miss a day, but your creatures stay.
